Ordinary Year

An ordinary year will be having 365 days in a year.

Leap Year

An ordinary year will be having 366 days in a year.

Odd Days

Number of days which are more than complete week in a given period of time.

Couting odd days

1 ordinary year = 365 days
= 52 weeks + 1 day
1 leap year = 366 days
= 52 weeks + 2 days

To find the year after which the calendar repeats after a year x, from year X, count years till the number of odd days is 0, the next year will have same calendar as X.
