Velocity or Speed

The speed of something in a given direction.
Speed = (1 / timetaken)
Speed= (Distance) / (Time Taken)

Distance

At a given amount of time and with the velocity the area covered is distance.
Distance = (Speed x Time taken)

Time

The time taken usually to cover certain distance.
Time Taken = (Distance) /(Speed)

So if a and b are speed which are in ratio a:b then its time taken is (1/a) : (1/b) or b:a
